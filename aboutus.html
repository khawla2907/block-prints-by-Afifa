Explore the Collection
Where it began

A love for print, pattern and heritage.

Block Prints by Afifa was created from a deep appreciation for handcrafted textiles and the beauty found in thoughtful details. Each collection is designed to feel distinctive while remaining easy to wear, style and cherish.

Based in Karachi, the brand reimagines traditional block printing through carefully selected colours, fabrics and motifs. The result is a collection that feels rooted in culture but made for modern everyday life.

“Every print carries the warmth of the hands that created it.”
Our Craft

Made slowly. Made thoughtfully.

Our process values patience, individuality and the small variations that make every hand-printed piece unique.

Thoughtful Design

Motifs, colour stories and fabric combinations are selected to create pieces that feel balanced, expressive and timeless.

Meet the founder

Designed with a personal point of view.

Afifa founded the brand with a simple purpose: to share the beauty of block-printed textiles in a way that feels personal, elegant and accessible.

Her creative direction blends a respect for traditional techniques with an instinct for colour and modern styling. Every collection reflects the belief that handcrafted pieces can be both culturally meaningful and naturally suited to contemporary homes and wardrobes.

— Afifa
