The Art Behind the Craft

Every motif begins with a hand-carved wooden block, dipped in natural dye and gently pressed onto cotton fabric. What follows is not just printing — it’s storytelling. It’s precision guided by patience.

From drying in open sunlight to layering each shade with care, this is slow fashion at its purest — rooted in heritage and brought to life by the hands of Afifa’s skilled artisans.
