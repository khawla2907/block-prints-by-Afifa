The Art Behind the Craft

Every motif begins with a hand-carved wooden block, dipped in natural dye and gently pressed onto cotton fabric. What follows is not just printing—it is storytelling, shaped through precision and patience.

From drying in open sunlight to layering each shade with care, this is slow fashion at its purest—rooted in heritage and brought to life by skilled hands.
